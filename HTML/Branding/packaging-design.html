Types of Packaging

Primary Packaging

Bottles

Used for liquids like beverages, oils, and chemicals.

Cans

Commonly used for beverages, food, and other consumables.

Blister Packs

Often used for pharmaceuticals, electronics, and small consumer goods.

Boxes and Cartons

Utilized for a wide range of products, including electronics, food, and cosmetics.

Tubes

Used for creams, gels, and other semi-liquid products.

Jars

Often used for cosmetics, food items like sauces and spreads, and other consumables.

Pouches

Flexible packaging used for snacks, liquids, and various other products.

Secondary Packaging

Corrugated Boxes

Used for shipping and transportation of primary packaged goods.

Shrink Wrap

Commonly used to bundle multiple items together.

Display Boxes

Designed for showcasing products on retail shelves.

Trays

Used to hold multiple units of a product, often found in food and beverage industries.

Tertiary Packaging

Pallets

Used for bulk shipping and handling of multiple packages.

Crates

Utilized for heavy or bulky items needing extra protection during transport.

Drums

Used for storing and transporting large quantities of liquids or bulk materials.

Specialized Packaging

Vacuum Packaging

Used to extend the shelf life of perishable goods by removing air from the package.

Aseptic Packaging

Sterile packaging used for products that need to be free from contamination, such as dairy products and juices.

Temperature-Controlled Packaging

Insulated packaging used for products that require a specific temperature range during transport, such as pharmaceuticals and perishable food items.

Eco-Friendly Packaging

Biodegradable Packaging

Made from materials that can break down naturally, such as plant-based plastics or paper.

Recyclable Packaging

Designed to be recycled after use, commonly made from materials like glass, aluminum, and certain plastics.

Reusable Packaging

Packaging that can be repurposed or used multiple times, such as glass jars or durable shopping bags.

Retail Packaging

Clamshell Packaging

Often used for electronics, toys, and small consumer goods.

Hanging Display Packaging

Designed to be hung on hooks in retail stores, common for small and lightweight products.

Gift Boxes and Bags

Attractive packaging used for gifting purposes.

Industrial Packaging

Bulk Bags (FIBCs)

Flexible Intermediate Bulk Containers used for storing and transporting large quantities of dry, flowable products.

Barrels

Used for transporting large volumes of liquids and solids.

Industrial Wraps and Films

Used to secure goods on pallets or protect products during storage and transport.
